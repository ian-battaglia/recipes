Eggplant Donburi

Another rice bowl dish, eggplant donburi or eggplant bowls offers an unagi-like eggplant fried with sauce, over rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 - Large Eggplant
  • Cornflour
  • 2 Cups - White Rice
  • Green Onions
  • Shoyu
  • Mirin
  • Sake

Steps

  1. Slice eggplant in half lengthwise, and then into slices.
  2. Coat eggplant in cornflour to prevent it from absorbing too much oil, and then fry in veggie oil.
  3. Once fried on both sides, place on paper towel on cutting board to remove excess oil.
  4. In a bowl, mix together sauce mixture of 2 tbps of shoyu, mirin, and sake.
  5. Replace eggplant in bowl, and add sauce to cook together.
  6. Watch careful to avoid burning the sauce.
  7. Serve over rice with sliced green onions and sesame seeds.
