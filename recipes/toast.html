Toast

You know what toast is.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Butter and/or Jam (Optional)
  • (You've got a toaster, right?)

Steps

  1. Insert bread into toaster.
  2. Select preferred doneness with toaster's knob.
  3. Once finished, remove from toaster, and add butter or jam if preferred.
  4. Enjoy!
